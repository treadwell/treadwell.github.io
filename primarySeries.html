  • dve: exhale, fold forward.
  • trini: inhale, head up only.
  • catvari: exhale, jump back to chaturanga.
  • panca: inhale, push up to upward dog.
  • sat: exhale, push back to downward dog.

    • Exhale. One.

  • nava: inhale, lift the arms up.
    • Exhale: samasthitih
